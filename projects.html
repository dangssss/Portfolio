01 · VNPost Churn Prediction System

End-to-end churn prediction system orchestrated with Airflow DAGs, deployed through Docker Compose with PostgreSQL as the analytical store.

  • Data pipeline: ZIP ingestion for four source tables with header/schema validation, SHA256 manifest tracking, full-refresh reloads, chunked Pandas processing, PostgreSQL COPY, and failed-file isolation.
  • Feature engineering: Static lifetime features and configurable K-month sliding-window behavior features in PostgreSQL, with checkpoint/resume and optimized recomputation for new windows.
  • Modeling output: XGBoost churn model promotion, monthly scoring, high-risk customer export, SHAP churn reasons, and rule-based fallback explanations.

02 · Customer Churn Prediction ver 2

Developing a Kubernetes-based second version of the VNPost churn system, with isolated Airflow tasks running in pods through KubernetesPodOperator.

  • Orchestration: Five Airflow DAGs across ingest, features, EDA, train, and score, deployed on a local kind cluster with Helm.
  • Dataset logic: Scope filtering, recency-based activity tiering, walk-forward window selection, EWMA trend computation, pseudo-labeling, label smoothing, and train-only StandardScaler fitting.
  • Operations: Guardrail-based model promotion, PSI/KS drift checks, backtest precision-in-list, Prometheus/Grafana monitoring, and custom PromQL alerts for XGBoost pods and Airflow scheduler health.

03 · Banking Churn Demo Pipeline

Internship project at FoxAI.JSC building a banking churn demo on 10,000 customer records, focused on model comparison, business-facing explanations, and calibrated confidence.

  • Feature work: Created balance-per-product and activity-per-tenure ratios, handled outliers, and prepared features for imbalanced churn classification.
  • Modeling: Benchmarked Random Forest, XGBoost, and LightGBM; LightGBM reached 0.9295 accuracy and 0.8142 churn-class F1.
  • Decision support: Applied SHAP for customer-level drivers and MAPIE conformal prediction with a 10% calibration split.

04 · Supply Chain Shipment Type Prediction

Scientific research project for medical supply chain logistics. My ML work focused on preparing shipment data, filling missing weight/freight fields, and predicting one of four shipment modes.

  • Data repair: Trained regression models to impute missing Weight (Kilograms) and Freight Cost (USD), making those fields usable for the downstream classifier.
  • Shipment prediction: Built a multiclass classifier for Air, Truck, Air Charter, and Ocean; XGBoost reached 0.9553 accuracy.
  • Uncertainty layer: Applied MAPIE conformal prediction to separate confident predictions from cases that should go to manual review.
